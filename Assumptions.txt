                     Assumption


1. Each user can register once and can link multiple bank accounts.
2. Each account belongs to exactly one branch.
3. Currency codes follow ISO-4217 standards (e.g., INR, USD, GBP).
4. Power of Attorney (POA) user must already exist in the system.
5. Each transaction is linked to one account only.
6. Audit logs capture only create, update, and delete actions.
7. Roles and permissions are pre-defined by the admin.
8. Term Deposit accounts inherit base Account properties but include maturity and interest details.
9. Account numbers are unique across all banks.
10. Bank employees have limited access only to their assigned branches.
